Basic energy

(Get the pun?) Basic energy is the stuff that powers up your Pokémon and lets it attack. There are 8 diffrent energy types. All of the energy types include Water, Metal, Dark, Psychic, Fire, Electric, and Fighting. Which energy you should put in your deck (more on decks later.) depends on your Pokémon's energy requirements (more on that later also.).

Special energy

Special energy is a certain type of energy with special qualities. Special energy can only be used on some of your Pokémon's energy requirements. Some Special energies like Luminous energy can be used on any of your Pokémon's requirements. The types of special energy include Luminous, Gift, Mist, Theraputic, Reversal, Jet, Boomerang, Treasure, ect. You don't want to always use Special energy, it just depends.
